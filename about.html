About

Built by practitioners of India's SME capital markets.

TGISME Capital Advisors LLP is the investment manager of Quantumgrowth Capital — a SEBI-registered Category III AIF. The team has spent three decades shaping how Indian SMEs access institutional capital and public markets.

01 · Our story

From platform to principal.

2012

The SME exchange is born.

Ajay Thakur architects India's first SME exchange at BSE — creating the country's first formal listing path for small and medium enterprises.

2012 – 2022

A decade of SME listings.

Under Ajay's leadership of the BSE SME & Startup segment, the platform scales to hundreds of listings, catalysing institutional participation in Indian SME capital.

2023 – 24

Quantumgrowth Capital.

TGISME Capital Advisors LLP is established. SEBI registers the fund as a Category III AIF — registration IN/AIF3/23-24/1473. The team now deploys capital through the rails it helped build.

Today

Investing alongside founders.

A concentrated book of Indian SMEs across manufacturing, services, tech-enabled operators, and healthcare. Capital, governance, and a route to public markets — deployed with institutional discipline.

02 · The founder

Ajay Thakur

Ajay Thakur
CEO & Managing Partner, TGIsmE · Founder, BSE SME platform

Ajay spent over two decades at BSE, where he conceived, launched, and led the SME platform from a white paper to a scaled public market. Under his leadership the segment listed hundreds of Indian SMEs and established a credible institutional exit route where none had previously existed.

At TGIsmE he invests through the rails he built — partnering with promoters who want the governance, board discipline, and listing-readiness that public markets reward.

04 · Values

What we hold ourselves to.

01

Conviction, not consensus.

We take positions we can defend to our unitholders — not positions that look easy in a committee memo.

02

Governance, before growth.

Boards, audits, and reporting aren't a tax on promoters — they're the foundation on which public-market value compounds.

03

Patience over velocity.

We deploy slowly, diligence relentlessly, and hold with intent. Speed of deployment is not a virtue in institutional investing.
