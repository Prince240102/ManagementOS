05 · Our approach

A four-step process, run with institutional discipline.

  1. 01

    Source

    Proprietary deal origination drawing on three decades of relationships across BSE SME, merchant bankers, and regional founder networks.

    Signals we prioritise

    Regional dominance, promoter continuity, clean books, listing-grade governance within 24 months.

  2. 02

    Diligence

    Unit economics, governance, and promoter-quality screens. Independent legal, financial, and on-site reviews before any capital is deployed.

    Third-party checks

    Independent audit, legal, channel checks, and on-ground operational review.

  3. 03

    Invest

    Concentrated positions at listing or growth stages. Structured to align incentives, protect downside, and support long holding periods.

    Structuring levers

    Primary and anchor tranches, board rights, milestone-linked deployment where appropriate.

  4. 04

    Partner

    Active post-investment engagement on governance, capital strategy, and access to public markets — we stay with founders through the build-out.

    Ongoing support

    Board governance, CFO bench-building, listing-readiness, and follow-on capital introductions.
