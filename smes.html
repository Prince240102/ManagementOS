02 · What we look for

The profile that earns our conviction.

Sector-agnostic within the Indian SME universe — but disciplined on promoter quality, unit economics, and governance readiness. A checklist, not a filter.

  • Revenue ₹{25}–{300} cr

    Profitable or near-profitable SMEs with two-to-three-year visibility on scaling growth.

  • Promoter quality

    Clean record, skin-in-the-game, willingness to build a board and formalise governance.

  • Defensible position

    Regional dominance, process IP, brand equity, or a sticky B2B relationship set that rivals struggle to copy.

  • Clean books

    Audited financials, organised tax position, and a CFO (in-house or fractional) willing to lift reporting to listing standard.

  • Listing readiness

    A plausible path to public-market visibility within 18–24 months of our entry — BSE SME or main-board.

  • Growth thesis

    A specific use-of-funds plan — capacity, distribution, M&A, or working capital — tied to a measurable outcome.

03 · Stages

Where we come in.

Growth

Primary capital, 18+ months before listing

Working capital, capacity expansion, strategic hires. We help put the governance rails in place well before the IPO roadshow.

Pre-IPO

Anchor and structured tranches

Bridge capital, pre-IPO rounds, and anchor participation in BSE SME or main-board issues with governance uplift baked into terms.

Listed

Post-listing support

Follow-on positions in listed SMEs where governance is demonstrable and a second act of growth is clearly funded.
